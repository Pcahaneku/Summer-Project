What we do

We offer swimming lessons for children aged 4 to 11, from beginners to advanced levels. Our small class sizes and experienced coaches ensure every learner thrives in a fun and supportive environment.

Our Swimming Grades

We offer a structured program across six grade levels, helping children build their skills step-by-step.

  • Grade 0: New to swimming, water confidence activities.
  • Grade 1: Basic stroke introduction and safety skills.
  • Grade 2: Developing stroke technique and stamina.
  • Grade 3: Improving efficiency in multiple strokes.
  • Grade 4: Advanced stroke skills and endurance.
  • Grade 5: Pre-competition skills and performance training.

Each class lasts one hour and are available on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday (4–5 pm, 5–6 pm, 6–7 pm) and on Saturday (2–3 pm, 3–4 pm).
